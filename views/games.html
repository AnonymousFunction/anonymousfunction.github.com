Games

Asteroids

A recreation of the Atari arcade Asteroids classic in HTML5 Canvas. Fly a spaceship and shoot asteroids before they destroy your ship!

Controls:
Turn Ship
Thruster
[ Space ] Shoot

Breakout

A recreation of the Atari arcade Breakout classic in HTML5 Canvas. A type of single-player tennis. Use your paddle to bounce the ball at the bricks to destroy them! Works on mobile devices.

Controls:
Move Paddle
[ Space ] Serve

Legend of Zelda

The classic action RPG from the Nintendo Entertainment System. Take Link through an array of dungeons and fight monsters to collect all the pieces of the Triforce and save Princess Zelda. Works on mobile devices. Supports Nintendo USB gamepad.

Controls:
Move Link
[ C ] B-Button Item
[ Space ] A-Button Item
[ Enter ] Menu
